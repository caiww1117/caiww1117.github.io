I am a postgraduate student at the School of Advanced Materials, Peking University, majoring in Materials Physics and Chemistry. I will be joining Hong Kong University of Science and Technology as a PhD student in Chemistry (Scientific Computation) in 2026.

My research focuses on leveraging first-principles calculations and data-driven AI approaches for energy materials discovery, including:

- **Computational Chemistry & Materials Science**: DFT calculations, ab initio molecular dynamics (AIMD) simulations
- **AI for Materials Discovery**: Large-scale literature mining, generative design of novel materials
- **CO₂ Reduction Reaction (eCO₂RR)**: Mechanism study and catalyst design
- **Renewable Battery**: Solid-state electrolytes and electrode materials

I am proficient in using VASP, Quantum ESPRESSO, and Gaussian for first-principles calculations, and have solid experience in Python programming, machine learning, and deep learning.
