Utilizing AIMD simulations to investigate the behavior of proton transportation in δ-MnO₂ with various interlayer distances and proton concentrations.

**Key Findings:**
- Studied the modulation of proton concentrations on the interlayer water molecules and environment of hydrogen bonds
- Found that increased interlayer distances and proton concentrations will lead to proton trapping and hinder proton transport
